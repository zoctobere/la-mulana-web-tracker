Notes for the user:

  • Optimal Capture Dimensions are 385px high x 500px wide
  • The map in the first row of ITEMS is the Shrine of the Mother Map.
  • Key Fairy Access (SOFTWARE) is an automatic tracker which will light up when the conditions are met.
    Requirements are: Isis Pendant (ITEMS), miracle.exe and mekuri.exe (SOFTWARE)
  • Bone Wall Removal (ITEMS) is an automatic tracker which will light up when the conditions are met.
    Bone Walls can be removed by one of the following:
    • collecting the Dragon Bone (USABLE ITEMS), yagomap.exe and yagostr.exe (SOFTWARE), and the Shrine of the Mother Map (ITEMS)
    • defeating all 8 Guardians
  • Progressive and counted items can be stepped back by right clicking. These items will also cycle forward to starting state.
    Progressive items include:
    • Whips (MAIN WEAPON)
    • Shields (SUB WEAPON)
    • Vessel/Medicine (USABLE ITEMS)
    • Diary/Talisman/Mulana Talisman (USABLE ITEMS)
    • Treasures/Anchor/Both (ITEMS)
    Counted items (COLL) include:
    • Maps (0 to 17)
    • Ankh Jewels (0 to 9)
    • Sacred Orbs (0 to 10)
